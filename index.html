Reaching out to under-privileged youth in the Houston area

Our Mission

The mission of BE A CHAMPION, INC. is to use sports mentoring, motivational speaking, education, and community service to enhance the development of our youth regardless of age, race, gender, religion, or creed. We hope that by doing so, our program will improve society as a whole, giving young people a chance at a better future.

Programs We Offer

DURING-SCHOOL PROGRAMS

Be A Champion, Inc. offers many during-school programming options as well as staffing. We specialize in tutorials for Testing and TAKS (Texas Assessment of Knowledge and Skills) using TEKS (Texas Essential Knowledge and Skills). These tutorials are staffed by Be A Champion, Inc. trained and approved tutors.

AFTER-SCHOOL AND SUMMER PROGRAMS

Be A Champion, Inc. offers over 90 customized programs that consist of enrichment and academics, servicing grades K-12. The after-school programs will operate from dismissal time – 6:00pm. This program allows students to enjoy enrichment activities while socializing with their friends after school.

CHAMPION FUEL FOOD PROGRAM

In May of 2014, BE A CHAMPION, INC. was awarded as a Contracting Entity/Sponsor with the Texas Department of Agriculture to provide the Child and Adult Care Food Program (CACFP) and the Summer Food Service Program (SFSP). These programs are designed to provide nutritious snacks and meals to students participating in enrichment programs across Texas. BE A CHAMPION, INC. supports these programs with after-school enrichment programs and staff. Snacks and meals consist of all natural, fresh ingredients with no preservatives provided by our partner, Revolution Foods, Inc. At-Risk CACFP snacks and meals can be served if: -At least 50% of your school location is approved for free and reduced lunch for all youth up to 18 years of age. -Schools, community centers, day care centers, apartments and churches are all qualified locations for service. Though the Champion Fuel Food Program, students perform better in school as they consume healthy, fresh, well-balanced meals each day along with homework assistance and after-school enrichment programs. LITTLE COOGS

Be A Champion Little Coogs Program began in 2002 in partnership with the University of Houston. The program caters to past, present, and future Cougars of today. As an outreach program, it organizes events and programs to youth providing exposure to a college campus and game day experience. The program is supported by generous sponsors and alumni from the University of Houston and corporate partners, allowing thousands of kids to attend UH sporting events each year. Through the program, we offer campus facility tours, academic incentive programs, drug awareness, motivational speaking, hospital visits, food drives and youth tailgating parties. Each game's tailgating party serves 1,500 kids.
